INTRO
-----
This is a total conversion of WWF Wrestlemania 2000 for N64 as what originally started as a project of what if AKI released WWF WarZone instead of Acclaim has turned into the teams love letter to such a popular time period in wrestling fans minds. We would love to have included every specific wrestler, themes, titantrons, costumes, arena, match type etc. but we are still restricted by the games limitations and we have tried to cram in as much into this project for you all to enjoy. 

Feedback and suggestions are welcome providing they are not just "you forgot this wrestler" or "why did you include this instead of this" or "the ring ropes are the wrong color". As a team we have weighed up options and had to make some sacrifices but still hope the game is enjoyable for you and brings you back to 1997-early 1998 so please be respectful of our creative decision.

THE ROSTER
----------

This roster is based around the early starts of the Attitude Era within 1997 WWF but some attire choices and CAW parts range from 1996 until March-April 1998

As for the unlockables. On the title screen, you will see several hints to unlock them. These are based on the original WWF Wrestlemania 2000 unlockables.

*Also clone one of the manager models go into edit mode and you may recognise that a certain Tay Tay made it in.


KNOWN GLITCHES AND BUGS
---------- 
While we are excited to present this release it does still have a few known graphical issues that we will address with further releases down the road or when this project is converted using SK Stylez edition or a further upcoming Expanded edition. 

Some of the notable graphical glitches are as follows;
- Kama Mustafa’s chest tattoo does not appear with the low plain singlet but can be shown if you use Vader’s and make it black on both color choices.
- Savio Vega has a chest tattoo however does not display correctly in game and if combined with certain upper body attire choices can result in a hole of transparency in your characters chest.
- When Phineas Godwinn taunts for a brief moment while his had changes to a different open hand texture the cuff of the attire model we originally removed shows briefly.
- When choosing certain attire parts when they are re-colored they do not react as intended.

We didn’t think these graphical glitches where enough to delay the release of this rom hack and we are aware of them and will look to correct them upon additional patch releases please be patient.
